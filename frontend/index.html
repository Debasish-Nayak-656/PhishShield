04 — REPORT CYBERCRIME

How to Report

If you've encountered or fallen victim to a phishing attack, report it immediately through these official Indian government channels.

🇮🇳
National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
cybercrime.gov.in
Official portal by Ministry of Home Affairs. Report all cybercrime complaints here including phishing, fraud, and online harassment.
→
📞
Cyber Crime Helpline
Call: 1930
National helpline for cybercrime victims. Available for immediate assistance with financial frauds, phishing, and online scams.
🛡️
CERT-In
cert-in.org.in
Indian Computer Emergency Response Team. Report phishing websites and malware. They coordinate with hosting providers for takedowns.
→
🏛️
I4C — Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre
i4c.mha.gov.in
Nodal body for coordinating cybercrime investigations across India. Works with state police forces.
→

🚨 If You Were Phished — Act Now

01
Disconnect — Go offline immediately to prevent further data exfiltration
02
Change Passwords — All accounts that use the same password as the compromised one
03
Contact Your Bank — Call immediately if financial credentials were involved
04
Report — File a complaint at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
05
Document — Take screenshots of the phishing page and all communications as evidence
